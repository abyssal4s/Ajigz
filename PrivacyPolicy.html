Privacy Policy

AJigz - Jigsaw Puzzle for Kids

Last Updated: May 2026

Introduction

Jigsaw Puzzle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, AJigz - Jigsaw Puzzle for Kids (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect device identifiers or tracking data
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect, upload, or share photos from your photo library — photos selected for custom puzzles are processed entirely on your device and never leave your device
  • We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies

Photos and Camera

The App requests access to your photo library only when you choose the "Custom Photos" feature. Photos you select are:

  • Processed and cropped locally on your device
  • Used solely to create puzzles within the App
  • Never uploaded, stored on our servers, or shared with any third party
  • Deleted from the App's memory when you close the puzzle

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional Premium upgrade as an in-app purchase. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment card details or financial information. Apple's own privacy policy applies to transaction data.

Children's Privacy

This App is designed for children ages 3 and up and is rated 4+ on the App Store. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • The App contains no advertising, no social sharing features, and no external links
  • No data is collected, transmitted, or shared through the App
  • The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable children's privacy laws

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or social networking services. There are no third-party SDKs that collect data.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of data breach involving your personal information through our App. All data used by the App remains on your device.

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and are not backed up to any server.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
